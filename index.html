About Us (::About Us)

Rwanda's greatest resource is its people, and Rwanda Reads encourages Rwandans to become literate. Being literate helps you in school, makes you valuable to employers, and helps you become the bright future of Rwanda. This website includes proverbs and stories in Kinyarwanda and English, as well as resources for learning English. Each story includes a short quiz (with answers included) testing how well you remember what you read. Quizzing yourself like this helps you learn English faster.
